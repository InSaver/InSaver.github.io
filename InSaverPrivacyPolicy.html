When you use the App, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage, including:

We do not collect, store, or have access to any of your private Instagram content, including but not limited to your login credentials, private messages, or non-public posts. The App only facilitates the download of publicly available content that you choose to save.

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

We will retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. We will retain and use your information to the extent necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.
